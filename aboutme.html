About Me

I'm a student in my fourth semester at Chippewa Valley Technical College finishing my Associates Degree in Software Development. I plan on graduating from the program in May, and then returning in the fall to finish the Mobile Web Development degree as well.

I like developing applications and websites in many different languages include ASP.NET, Visual Basic and Java. I'm also well established in the use of HTML5, CSS, Javascript, and JQuery.

I have several projects in working progress. Many done throughout my college experience with varying levels of knowledge and understanding. I anticipate revisiting these for improvements both for myself and for the code as time permits.

I'm currently looking for interesting open-source projects to participate in, and have become familiar with the use of version control through Github.

I also enjoy designing projects for the Arduino in my spare time. The number of possibilities that a person can make is incredible to me, and I've always enjoyed the software/hardware interaction through automation. Much of my past employment history working for Cummins exposed me to the use of automation and sparked my interest in make projects for myself.
